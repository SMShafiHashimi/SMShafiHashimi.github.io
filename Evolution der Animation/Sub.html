Die Handlung in den Animes/Mangas mit dem Genre Action (eng. Begriff für Handeln) ist geprägt von Kämpfen, Verfolgungen und Gewalt/Chaos. Sie besitzen viel Spannung und es herrscht eine sehr dynamische Handlung (beweglich/schnelllebig). https://animanga.fandom.com/de/wiki/Kategorie:Action

Mashle: Magic and Muscles (Japanese: マッシュル-MASHLE-, Hepburn: Masshuru) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Kōmoto. It was serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump from January 2020 to July 2023, with its chapters collected in 18 tankōbon volumes. An anime television series adaptation produced by A-1 Pictures aired from April to July 2023. A second season premiered in January 2024. https://www.crunchyroll.com/de/series/GDKHZEP8W/mashle-magic-and-muscles

Mob Psycho 100 (Japanese: モブサイコ100, Hepburn: Mobu Saiko Hyaku) is a Japanese web manga series written and illustrated by One. It was serialized on Shogakukan's Ura Sunday website from April 2012 to December 2017. It has been also available online on Shogakukan's mobile app MangaONE since December 2014. Shogakukan compiled its chapters in 16 tankōbon volumes. The story follows Shigeo Kageyama, nicknamed Mob, a boy who has strong psychic powers, and his struggles to find the simple happiness he is looking for. https://www.crunchyroll.com/de/series/GY190DKQR/mob-psycho-100
